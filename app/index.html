tl&dr

I am Stéphane (pronounced Stéphane), I play guitar and computer keyboards. I was born and raised in Montpellier, southern France, but currently reside in London. I pay for my Nutella by doing web and mobile development.

Story of my life

I was born at some point in 1990, and I don't regret my decision. I grew up in Montpellier, got a Computer Sciences BS (as in: Bachelor of Sciences) and then real life started.

I did some open-source work with GNOME for a little while, and somehow this got me an internship in San Mateo, California with the awesome people at Fanhattan. I mean, when I was there they were awesome. I don't know now. They probably still are, but you never know.

Then I got back to France and I got a job in Paris with Plizy. It was a lot of fun and buttery croissants, but then they decided to move to San Francisco, California, so I grabbed my suitcase, tried to grow a beard, and moved there as well. And I got a fixie.

sept 2013I moved on to Spinlister/Liquid, wonderful team, great project, stupid visa. Had to retreat to France after two months, because I was just an "authorized alien" in the US. Give me your tired, your poor, but come back in two years.

I played sleeping contests with my cat back in Montpellier.

march 2013I moved to London and joined the team at Lyst. I was in charge of web development, but something was wrong in the equation and I was free again after two months. Learned a tremendous amount of stuff there. Did you know that dolphins can group-rape other dolphins? Me neither. And also Javascript code (it doesn't *have* to be ugly) and Django magic.
