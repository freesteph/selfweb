Synopsis

I am a French software engineer passionnate about making pixels come together, programming, designing, head-banging and other various activities.

Life

I was born the same day as Julius Caesar, but because of unfortunate circumstances I haven't conquered Italy yet, however I do share the special habit of walking half-naked in my house.

As a kid I successively dreamed about becoming a Formula One pilot, an inventor, a masseur and a rock-star.

Apart from the rock star part which I am currently working on, I was able to achieve a part of each of those dreams in my daily developer duty, designing and building systems that will make people's life easier.

I love the Beatles.
