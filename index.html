« Computer science is no more about computers than astronomy is about telescopes. »

So just because I graduated in Computer Sciences doesn't mean I code everyday from my basement or that I dream binary.

But I do like coding. Not only because it helps me enjoy problem-solving, but also because it is one of those activites that grabs my full attention, drives all of my focus toward one ultimate goal: you.

I care about details. The best hope I have for whatever comes out of my keyboard is that it will fullfil its mission, improve the current situation and give you pleasure to work with. I want the world to stop thinking of computers as a source of frustration.
